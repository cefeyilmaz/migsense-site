🗑️ Delete My Data

Last updated: July 2, 2026

MigSense does not require you to create an account, so there is no traditional "account" to delete. Instead, you can permanently delete all of the tracking data we hold on your device (and in your own private iCloud, if sync is on) at any time from within the app.

Delete From Within the App

To delete your data:

  1. Open MigSense
  2. Go to the Settings tab
  3. In the Privacy section, tap "Delete My Data"
  4. Confirm the deletion in the confirmation dialog

What Gets Deleted

  • All of your attack records (start/end time, severity, symptoms, triggers, notes)
  • Weather and Apple Health snapshots attached to your attack records
  • Your medication intake logs
  • Your daily check-ins
  • Your disability/impact assessments
  • Your AI chat history and cached insights

If iCloud sync is on, this deletion is also reflected in your own private iCloud database.

What Is Kept

So the app remains usable after deletion, your own custom trigger, symptom, relief-method, and medication catalog (just the name lists, not your history) is kept. You can edit or remove these separately from Settings.

Deleting the App

Removing MigSense from your device deletes the local copy. If you previously turned on iCloud sync, you can also remove the copy in your private iCloud database via Settings (iOS) → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage, then select MigSense and delete its data.

Server-Side Records

If you used the AI features, usage records (such as your monthly request count) tied to an anonymous user identifier are kept server-side to enforce your quota. These records cannot be linked back to your personal information, but if you'd like them deleted as well, contact us at the email below with any details you have (approximate usage date, device/platform) so we can process your request.

Don't Forget Your Subscription

Deleting your data does not cancel an active MigSense Pro subscription. To cancel your subscription: App Store → Profile → Subscriptions → MigSense → Cancel Subscription.
